## 8. FAQ Section

### Is this a green dye?
No. This product is not a dye or temporary color spray. It is designed to support lawn and soil recovery.

### How fast will I see results?
Grass recovery depends on severity, grass type, soil condition, weather, and watering. Some spots may improve with care, while severely dead patches may need reseeding.

### Is it safe around pets?
Use according to label directions. Keep pets off treated areas until the product has dried or watered in as directed.

### Can I use it with a sprayer?
Yes, it can be applied with a pump sprayer, watering can, or compatible lawn sprayer when mixed according to label directions.

### Will it stop my dog from peeing in the same spot?
This product is designed for lawn and soil recovery. It is not a behavioral training product.
