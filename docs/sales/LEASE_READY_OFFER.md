# Lease-Ready Offer

## Product name

**Product Video Automation Engine**

## One-line pitch

Turn a product catalog into branded Amazon, Shopify, Walmart, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and website-ready product videos.

## Best first customer

Start with ecommerce product companies that already sell online and need more product videos:

- Amazon sellers
- Shopify brands
- Walmart Marketplace sellers
- Small manufacturers
- Agencies managing ecommerce brands
- Lawn, garden, pet, cleaning, home, tool, farm, beauty, and consumer product brands

## Recommended offer structure

### Setup fee

$2,500-$7,500 per brand deployment.

Setup includes:

- Product spreadsheet / catalog mapping
- Brand voice and claim-rule setup
- Logo, colors, CTA, and website configuration
- 3-5 video templates
- 5 approval sample videos
- Dry-run campaign validation
- Basic staff handoff

### Monthly license

$799-$2,500 per month.

Monthly license includes:

- Continued system access
- Monthly template updates
- Product feed support
- Basic monitoring
- Bug fixes
- A defined monthly video generation allowance

### Agency / white-label license

Custom quote: $7,500-$25,000 setup plus $2,000-$5,000 per month.

Use only when the customer has multiple brands, multiple client accounts, or wants agency resale rights.

## What the client gets

- A configured workflow for their brand
- Product feed template
- Script generation workflow
- Video generation workflow
- Caption and hashtag output
- Platform-specific export settings
- Dry-run validation process
- Rights to use generated videos for their own brand, subject to the signed agreement

## What Nature's Way Soil keeps

- Ownership of the core software
- Ownership of reusable workflows and templates
- Ownership of generic prompt logic and automation structure
- Right to license similar systems to other companies
- Right to improve and reuse non-client-specific code

## Recommended license terms

Use these business terms as talking points. A lawyer should convert them into a final contract.

- Non-exclusive license
- Non-transferable license
- No source-code resale
- No sublicensing without written approval
- Client owns/provides its product data and brand assets
- Client is responsible for claim accuracy and platform compliance
- No guaranteed sales or ad performance
- Payment due monthly in advance
- Access ends if payment stops
- Client must keep credentials secure
- Client may not use the system for illegal, infringing, or deceptive content

## Simple sales script

"We built a product-video automation system for our own ecommerce brand because creating enough Amazon and social videos manually was too slow and expensive. Now we install that same type of system for product companies. Your team provides a product sheet, images, and brand rules. The system generates scripts, short-form videos, captions, and platform-ready versions for Amazon, Shopify, Walmart, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and your website."
