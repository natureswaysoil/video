# Nature's Way Soil Website Conversion Funnel

## Goal
Use YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, email, and retargeting to drive buyers to natureswaysoil.com so Nature's Way Soil keeps more margin instead of sending every customer to Amazon.

## Funnel Overview

### 1. Traffic Sources
Primary channels:
- YouTube Shorts
- Instagram Reels
- Facebook Reels
- Facebook Groups
- Email
- Meta retargeting ads

Optional later:
- Pinterest after approval
- Google Search / Shopping
- Amazon as discovery and review trust, not the only checkout

### 2. Core Offer
Use a direct-site offer that Amazon cannot easily match:
- Bundle savings
- Free guide / soil recovery PDF
- Subscribe-and-save refill offer
- First-order discount
- Farm-direct small business positioning

Recommended first offer:
"Order direct and save with soil-first lawn & garden bundles."

### 3. Landing Page Structure
Each traffic video should send visitors to a product-specific landing page.

Page sections:
1. Hero section
   - Product name
   - One problem-focused headline
   - 1-sentence benefit promise
   - Product image or video
   - Primary CTA: Order Direct
   - Secondary CTA: Get the Soil Guide

2. Problem section
   - Explain the customer pain
   - Keep it simple and visual
   - Example: yellow dog spots, thin grass, weak soil, poor blooms, dry raised beds

3. Product solution section
   - What the product is
   - Why it works from the soil up
   - 3 core benefits

4. How to use section
   - Simple steps
   - Dilution rate
   - Coverage
   - Frequency
   - Best timing

5. Bundle section
   - Single bottle
   - 2-pack or refill bundle
   - Seasonal care bundle
   - Best value badge on bundle

6. Trust section
   - Small family business
   - Made by Nature's Way Soil & Vermicompost LLC
   - Soil biology focused
   - Safe-use positioning where appropriate
   - Reviews/testimonials

7. FAQ section
   - How often do I apply it?
   - Is it safe around pets/kids after label-directed use?
   - Can I use it with a hose-end sprayer?
   - How long before I see improvement?
   - What grass/plants is it for?

8. Final CTA
   - Order direct from Nature's Way Soil
   - Get bundle savings
   - Capture email if not ready to buy

### 4. Email Capture
Every product page should have one simple lead magnet:
- Dog product: Free Yellow Spot Lawn Recovery Guide
- Humic/Kelp: Free Soil Revival Guide
- Compost/Biochar: Free Living Soil Starter Guide
- Fruit Tree/Bone Meal: Free Bloom & Root Feeding Guide
- Hay/Pasture: Free Pasture Recovery Checklist

Popup or embedded offer:
"Get the free soil recovery guide + direct-order offers."

Fields:
- Email
- Optional: what are you trying to fix? lawn, garden, trees, pasture, dog spots

### 5. Email Flow

Email 1: Delivery + education
Subject: Your Soil Recovery Guide
CTA: View recommended product

Email 2: Problem awareness
Subject: The problem usually starts below the surface
CTA: Watch the short product video

Email 3: Product solution
Subject: A simple soil-first fix
CTA: Order direct

Email 4: Bundle push
Subject: Best value: seasonal care bundle
CTA: Buy bundle

Email 5: Trust / founder note
Subject: Why we make it this way
CTA: Support a small soil-focused business

### 6. Retargeting
Retarget these audiences:
- Website visitors in last 14 days
- Product page viewers, no purchase
- Add-to-cart, no checkout
- Video viewers from Facebook/Instagram
- Email clickers, no purchase

Ad sequence:
1. Reminder ad: Still dealing with the same soil/lawn problem?
2. Proof ad: Soil-first care, simple to apply
3. Bundle ad: Save when you order direct
4. Urgency ad: Stock up for the season

### 7. Analytics Events
Track these events:
- view_product
- view_video
- lead_capture
- add_to_cart
- begin_checkout
- purchase
- guide_download
- bundle_click

UTM naming:
- utm_source=youtube / instagram / facebook / email
- utm_medium=organic_shorts / organic_reels / organic_groups / email / paid_social
- utm_campaign=category-product-slug
- utm_content=hook-name or video-id

Do not make social traffic choose between Amazon and the website. Send social traffic to the website first. Use Amazon as backup trust only, not the main CTA.
