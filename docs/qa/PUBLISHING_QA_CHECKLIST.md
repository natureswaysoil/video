# Publishing QA Checklist

Use this checklist before any generated video is posted to YouTube, Instagram, X/Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn, or embedded on the Nature's Way Soil website.

## 1. Product and offer clarity

- [ ] The video promotes one clear buyer pain, not every product benefit at once.
- [ ] The product name matches the website, Amazon listing, and Google Sheet source row.
- [ ] The call to action is specific: buy now, request quote, request pilot, download capability statement, or book a call.
- [ ] The destination URL is live and does not route to a placeholder page.
- [ ] The video does not make unverified fertilizer, pesticide, disease-control, or guaranteed-result claims.

## 2. Script quality

- [ ] The first 3 seconds name the problem or buyer pain.
- [ ] The script includes the product mechanism in plain English.
- [ ] The script includes one trust signal: family farm, HUBZone, BioPreferred, made fresh weekly, pet-safe, or GPC-ready.
- [ ] The script uses simple customer language, not internal formulation language.
- [ ] The final line contains a clear CTA.

## 3. Accessibility and captions

- [ ] A plain transcript is saved in `content/transcripts/`.
- [ ] A caption file is saved in `content/captions/` as `.srt` or `.vtt`.
- [ ] Captions are checked manually for spelling, product names, and timing.
- [ ] Captions include important non-speech audio when relevant, such as `[music]`.
- [ ] On-screen text does not duplicate captions in a confusing way.

## 4. Visual quality

- [ ] Logo is readable on mobile.
- [ ] On-screen text stays inside the safe area for vertical and horizontal exports.
- [ ] No text is placed under platform UI areas where captions, buttons, or usernames display.
- [ ] B-roll matches the product and does not show a misleading crop, lawn, animal, or application method.
- [ ] Before/after or result imagery is accurate and not exaggerated.

## 5. Audio quality

- [ ] Voice is clear and not covered by background music.
- [ ] No clipping, harsh peaks, or silent gaps.
- [ ] AI voice pronunciation is checked for product names: Nature's Way Soil, humic, fulvic, biochar, yucca, kelp, BM-1, HUBZone.
- [ ] Pace is natural enough for commercial buyers to understand.

## 6. Thumbnail and metadata

- [ ] Thumbnail has one main message, not multiple competing headlines.
- [ ] Thumbnail text is readable at mobile size.
- [ ] YouTube title is under 100 characters.
- [ ] Description includes buyer segment, product mechanism, CTA, and URL.
- [ ] Tags include product type, buyer type, and brand name.
- [ ] UTM tracking is added when linking to website landing pages.

## 7. Procurement and B2B trust check

- [ ] Government/public-sector videos mention HUBZone or GPC-ready only when appropriate.
- [ ] Quote/invoice/ACH language is accurate.
- [ ] Contact email matches the website footer and contact page.
- [ ] Capability statement or procurement packet links work.

## 8. Final approval

- [ ] Video reviewed by owner or designated reviewer.
- [ ] Metadata reviewed against the source Google Sheet.
- [ ] Published URL written back to the sheet.
- [ ] Date, platform, campaign, and product SKU recorded.
