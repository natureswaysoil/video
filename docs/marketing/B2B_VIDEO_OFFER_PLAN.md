# B2B Video Offer Plan

The best commercial use of this repo is not selling generic AI videos. The strongest offer is a repeatable video-and-outreach engine that sells Nature's Way Soil soil recovery products to public-sector and commercial grounds buyers.

## Primary positioning

**Nature's Way Soil is a HUBZone small business supplying biobased, soil-first turf and grounds recovery products for public-sector buyers and commercial grounds crews.**

## Best initial buyer segments

| Segment | Pain point | Best video angle | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Landscapers and grounds contractors | Need visible results and repeatable treatment programs | Turn one treatment into a recurring service | Request crew pricing |
| Municipal parks and public works | Stressed parks, medians, public turf, and budget pressure | Improve soil health before replacing turf | Request pilot quote |
| Schools and campuses | Safe, visible grounds care around students and families | Better soil for school grounds and athletic areas | Request school grounds pilot |
| Golf and recreation | Turf quality, root stress, and water pressure | Liquid biochar and humic/kelp recovery support | Request recreation quote |
| Base housing, kennels, and pet areas | Dog urine spots and high-use turf damage | Repair soil balance in pet-use areas | Request pet-area kit |

## Flagship video stack

Create three reusable videos instead of one generic brand video.

1. **B2B explainer, 30-45 seconds**
   - Buyer pain: stressed turf, compacted soil, high-maintenance areas.
   - Mechanism: liquid biochar, humic/fulvic, kelp, yucca, beneficial biology.
   - CTA: request a pilot quote.

2. **Proof clip, 20-30 seconds**
   - Show application, before/after, root/soil animation, or customer use case.
   - CTA: see recovery program.

3. **Trust/procurement clip, 20-30 seconds**
   - HUBZone, GPC-ready, invoice/ACH support, family-farm manufacturing.
   - CTA: download capability statement or request procurement packet.
