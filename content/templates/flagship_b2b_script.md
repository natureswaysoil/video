# Flagship B2B Script Template

Use this for 30-45 second public-sector, contractor, school, golf, and grounds-maintenance videos.

- Product:
- Target buyer:

- Landing page:

## Script structure

### 0-3 seconds: Problem hook

`[Target buyer pain] costs crews time, water, and repeat labor.`

Examples:

- `Compacted turf and stressed grass cost grounds crews time, water, and repeat labor.`
- `Dog spots and high-use pet areas can make public green spaces look neglected fast.`
- `Tired school grounds need more than a quick green-up.`

### 3-12 seconds: Product introduction

`Nature's Way Soil helps [buyer] support healthier soil with [product/mechanism].`

### 12-25 seconds: Mechanism and benefit

`This formula combines [ingredients] to support [plain-English outcomes].`

Allowed benefit language examples:

- supports stronger roots
- supports better soil moisture movement
- helps improve nutrient holding
- supports microbial activity in the root zone
- helps stressed turf recover as part of a proper watering and mowing program

Avoid unsupported language:

- guaranteed green-up
- cures disease
- kills weeds
- replaces all fertilizer
- instant repair
- pesticide claims unless registered and approved

### 25-35 seconds: Trust signal

`We are a family farm / HUBZone small business / GPC-ready supplier / made fresh weekly.`

### Final 5 seconds: CTA

`Request a pilot quote and put living soil to work on your grounds.`
