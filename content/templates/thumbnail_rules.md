# Thumbnail Rules

Use thumbnails to make the buyer understand the pain and product in one glance.

## Core rules

1. Use one message per thumbnail.
2. Keep text under 45 characters.
3. Make the product or result visible.
4. Avoid clutter, tiny ingredient lists, and long claims.
5. Make mobile readability the standard.
6. Do not imply guaranteed results unless you have proof and approved claim language.

- Repair Stressed Turf Naturally
- Liquid Biochar for Public Grounds
- Better Soil for Parks & Schools
- Soil Recovery for Public Spaces

### Amazon/DTC

- Stronger Roots Start Here

## Thumbnail QA

- [ ] Text readable at 25% size.
- [ ] Brand/logo visible but not overpowering.
- [ ] Product bottle or use case visible.
- [ ] No misleading before/after image.
- [ ] CTA is not fighting headline text.
- [ ] Works in 16:9 and 9:16 crop zones when needed.
